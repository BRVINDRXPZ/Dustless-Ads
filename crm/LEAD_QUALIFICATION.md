# Lead Qualification

> **Planning specification — inactive.** No live accounts, credentials, communications, or real customer data.

## Purpose

Consistent fit assessment without discriminatory rules.

## Framework

| Segment | Factors | Output |
|---|---|---|
| Residential | service, geography, approximate square footage, timeline, occupancy, condition, budget readiness, photo availability, seriousness, operational fit | qualified / disqualified / pending; reason and human notes |
| Contractor | company, project type, scope clarity, plans, bid due date, schedule, location, repeat potential, payment process, relationship value | account and opportunity status; risks and next action |
| Commercial | property type, square footage, occupancy, schedule, phasing, vendor/insurance/contract requirements, payment terms, operational risk | readiness, opportunity status, risk review |

## Method

Collect only necessary project facts. Do not infer protected traits, use neighborhood as a proxy, or discriminate. Unknown answers remain pending. Budget readiness means willingness to discuss scope/economics—not profiling. Human reviews edge cases and records evidence.

## Exit

Qualified requires owner-approved service/geography/operational fit and a real next step. Disqualification requires an approved reason; price-only/too-small thresholds remain TBD. Safety, contract and technical judgments remain human.
