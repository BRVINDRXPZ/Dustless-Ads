# Deduplication Policy

> **Planning specification — inactive.** No live accounts, credentials, communications, or real customer data.

## Purpose

Exact ID first; then normalized email/phone plus contextual account/project/date. Fuzzy name/address only flags candidates. Never auto-merge. Steward compares evidence, selects survivor, preserves aliases/relationships and logs before/after/reason.

## Control

Owner approval, audit logging, least privilege, and documented exception handling are mandatory.
