# Local SEO Strategy

## Objective and guardrails
Make NOLA Dustless discoverable for qualified hardwood-flooring demand across owner-approved Greater New Orleans and Northshore markets by aligning accurate entity data, useful pages, real project proof, ethical reviews, and legitimate local relationships. **Repository facts:** the public name is documented as NOLA Dustless Hardwood Floors; the website is `https://www.noladustless.com/`; Greater New Orleans is primary and the Northshore secondary. Services and advantages remain preliminary and require verification.

No rank, review count, GBP configuration, category availability, business address, hours, service area, credential, or platform feature is asserted. This phase changed no public system.

## Operating model
1. **Truth layer:** owner-approved NAP, services, areas, credentials, access, and permission register.
2. **Entity layer:** audit GBP and authoritative citations against that truth layer; change only after separate approval.
3. **Experience layer:** distinct service/local pages with useful local expertise and proof; integrate with the Phase 4 prototype and Phase 5 measurement spec before publishing.
4. **Proof layer:** permissioned project media and an all-customer, no-incentive review workflow.
5. **Authority layer:** editorially legitimate partnerships, coverage, memberships, supplier profiles, and referrals—never purchased links.
6. **Measurement layer:** manual non-personalized baseline, Search Console, GBP performance, CRM outcomes, and monthly reconciliation after access approval.

## Category strategy (proposal; validate current)
- **Primary candidate:** the closest current GBP category specifically describing a wood-flooring contractor. Do not assume the label or availability; inspect the live category selector and official guidance on the execution date.
- **Secondary candidates:** only current categories that exactly match verified, routinely delivered installation, refinishing, or contractor activity. Categories are not keyword slots.
- **Avoid:** generic categories chosen only for reach, services not actually delivered, retailer/showroom categories without a qualifying operation, and repeated category changes.
- **Test:** export baseline; record category/version/date; verify eligibility and competitors only as observations; obtain approval; change one variable; hold other fields stable; monitor discovery, actions, qualified leads and anomalies for at least a full comparable reporting period; revert on relevance/quality deterioration.
- **Risks:** reverification, visibility volatility, misleading representation, changed leads, or policy action. No category change is authorized here.
- **Validation:** live owner-controlled GBP UI + dated official Google documentation + actual business model + owner confirmation; log evidence URL/screenshot/date.
