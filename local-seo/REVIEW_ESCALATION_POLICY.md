# Review Escalation Policy

## Triage
| Level | Trigger | Internal target (proposal) | Owner |
|---|---|---|---|
| 0 routine | positive/constructive, no sensitive issue | draft/respond within 2 business days | trained profile manager |
| 1 service recovery | dissatisfaction, schedule, communication, quality expectation | triage within 1 business day | owner/operations |
| 2 sensitive | safety, injury, property damage, discrimination, threats, payment dispute, legal/regulatory allegation, media inquiry | preserve; no substantive public response until counsel/insurer-directed review | owner + appropriate counsel/insurer |
| 3 integrity | suspected fake, extortion, conflict, impersonation, coordinated activity | capture evidence; validate current platform reporting policy; neutral holding response if approved | owner/profile lead |

## Procedure
Capture public URL, exact content, display name, timestamp and screenshots; never add secrets/customer data to this repo. Match privately against records using least privilege. Acknowledge without admitting liability. Move genuine resolution offline, log commitments and follow through. Do not condition resolution on editing/removing a review. Report only when a current platform rule appears violated, not because criticism is negative. One spokesperson; no crew replies.

## Stop conditions
Do not respond while facts are disputed, emotions are high, legal/insurance notification may be required, or identity cannot be safely verified. Never disclose the customer’s address, scope, messages, payment, photos or alleged conduct publicly. Document approval and final response; review patterns monthly.
