## Weekly control meeting
Review unresolved owner inputs, evidence/permissions, capacity, risks, QA exceptions and exact approvals. No silence or roadmap inclusion constitutes approval. If a dependency is missing, complete research/templates and keep execution blocked rather than guessing.

## Day-90 exit gate
Approved NAP; dated GBP/citation evidence; validated category/service proposal; staging-quality core pages; tested ethical review/photo SOPs; evidence-gated geographic decisions; baseline/report design; explicit implementation backlog. Public systems remain untouched unless separately authorized outside this phase.
