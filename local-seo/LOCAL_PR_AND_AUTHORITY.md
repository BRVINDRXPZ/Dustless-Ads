## Approval workflow and success
Score relevance, evidence readiness, distinctiveness, customer/privacy risk, operational timing and commercial audience. Owner approves target, angle and any contact. Measure accurate mentions, qualified referrals, relationship progression, permissioned links and reusable proof—not “coverage” at any cost. Correct errors promptly through an approved process.
