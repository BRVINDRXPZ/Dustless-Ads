# Ad Testing Plan

**Unit of success:** qualified opportunity and downstream value, not RSA asset labels alone. Two concepts per launch ad group are supplied in `responsive_search_ads.csv`.

| Test | Audiences/campaigns | Concept A | Concept B | Primary readout | Gate |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Dust control vs restoration | Refinishing | Reduced-mess process | Restore/renew decision | Qualified lead and scope mix | Dust wording approved |
| Local authority vs transformation | Refinishing/installation | Local hardwood focus | Visible before/after outcome | Qualified estimate rate | Avoid authority/superiority claim |
| Restore before replace vs appearance | Repair/refinishing | Save viable wood, where feasible | Renew worn appearance | Repairability and win quality | Feasibility caveat |
| Specialist vs general flooring | Core services | Hardwood-focused | Plain service expertise | Wrong-service rate; value | “Specialist” approval |
| Phone-first vs form-first | All residential | Call to discuss | Request assessment | Contact + qualified rate | Answering capacity/privacy |
| Free estimate vs professional assessment | Residential | Complimentary only if approved | Professional assessment | Quality, burden, booked rate | Fee/output owner decision |
| Reliability vs technical expertise | Contractor | Scope/coordination | Plan/detail review | Valid project packages | Operations proof |
| Accountability vs visual quality | Commercial | Schedule/scope controls | Finished environment | Walkthroughs and pipeline value | Commercial proof/capacity |
| Core vs Northshore | Separate campaigns | Metro pages/rules | Town-specific pages/rules | Qualified CAC and travel-adjusted GP | Territory/minimums |

## Method

Pre-register audience, hypothesis, one changed concept, eligible traffic, primary metric, guardrails, minimum evidence basis, conversion lag, and stop rule. Use ad variations/experiments where currently supported. Avoid unnecessary pinning; pin only legally necessary identity/qualifier text after previewing combinations. Inspect combinations for truth and grammar. Do not infer asset causality from platform labels alone.

## RSA provisional limits

Production files assume up to 15 headlines (30 characters each) and 4 descriptions (90 characters each), with at least 3 headlines and 2 descriptions. These common limits were **not live-verified** because web access failed and must be validated before import. Paths are modeled at 15 characters. Automated validation checks local text lengths only.
