# Performance Max Readiness

**Recommendation:** do not use Performance Max as a primary launch campaign.

## Required gates

- Primary call/form conversions are configured, deduplicated, and QA’d.
- Qualified-lead status and disqualification reasons are measured.
- Estimate, proposal, win, revenue, and preferably gross profit can be imported and corrected.
- Search has produced usable conversion and query-quality data across conversion lag.
- Approved, correctly licensed logos, images, and videos exist in required current formats.
- Final URLs, brand rules, audience signals, exclusions, and account-level negative controls are understood in the current platform.
- Capacity, budget, service areas, and lead-response ownership are approved.

## Staged recommendation

After all gates pass, run a geographically isolated, budget-capped incrementality test using proven services and value signals. Keep Brand Search protected and monitor brand cannibalization, final URLs, placements/insights available, lead quality, and offline value. Compare incremental qualified opportunities and gross profit—not platform-reported conversions alone. Stop if traffic cannot be explained, wrong-service/geography increases, value data degrades, or Search loses controllable profitable coverage.

## Asset rules

No stock imagery implying NOLA work, no unpermissioned projects/customers, no unsupported credentials, and no auto-created text/URL expansion until reviewed. Current asset counts, dimensions, video behavior, brand exclusions, and negative controls require live platform verification.
