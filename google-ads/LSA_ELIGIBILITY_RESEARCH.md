3. Record available category wording, geography, required license/insurance types, screening party, costs, billing model, and verification steps with URL/date/screenshot.
4. Confirm legal entity, license holder, insurance, phone, address/service-area status, and Google Business Profile alignment with owner records.
5. Review current LSA policies, review linkage, call recording, message/booking settings, lead dispute/credit process, responsiveness requirements, and advertiser verification.
6. Obtain owner/legal approval before submission; never upload credentials or private documents to this repository.

## Decision rule

If an exact flooring/refinishing category is available and the business satisfies every requirement, model LSA as a separate controlled channel. Measure charged lead, qualified lead, booked estimate, win, revenue, and gross profit. If only a misleading category is available, requirements cannot be met, or quality is not auditable, do not launch.
