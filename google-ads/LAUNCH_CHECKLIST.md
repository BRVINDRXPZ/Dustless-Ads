# Launch Checklist

No item authorizes publishing, spend, account connection, or live changes. Obtain explicit owner approval after every required box passes.

## Business and operations

- [ ] Approved services, exclusions, minimum project sizes, geographies, Northshore rules, capacity, response hours, budget, and economic ceilings.
- [ ] Approved residential/B2B qualification and disqualification rules; lead owner and escalation path.
- [ ] Claims register supports every public phrase; “dust-free” absent; credentials/proof/phone/address verified.
- [ ] Landing pages exist for each launched intent and accurately explain offer, scope, constraints, privacy, and CTA.

## Measurement

- [ ] Primary and secondary actions configured as specified; test events deduplicated and excluded from bidding.
- [ ] Call tracking, forms, consent, UTMs/auto-tagging, lead IDs, CRM stages, offline imports, values, and corrections pass QA.
- [ ] Campaign-specific goals contain only appropriate primaries; weak engagement actions are secondary.
- [ ] Reconciliation owner, dashboard, response SLA, and search-term log exist.

## Current-platform validation (required because live web verification failed)

- [ ] Official Google documentation rechecked for RSA text/count/path limits and combination behavior.
- [ ] Current Google Ads Editor CSV headers, status values, match syntax, negatives, assets, locations, and conversion import workflow mapped in preview.
- [ ] Current availability/behavior of Search Partners, Display expansion, Manual CPC, Maximize Clicks cap, Smart Bidding, broad match, campaign goals, and location presence option confirmed.
- [ ] Asset requirements checked: business name/logo verification, call, location, images, sitelinks, callouts, snippets, lead form, price, promotion, policies, formats, counts, and character limits.
- [ ] Advertiser verification, billing, identity, destination, consent, call recording, customer match/enhanced/offline data, and personalized-ad policies reviewed.
- [ ] LSA category/geography and requirements recorded from official eligibility flow; no eligibility claimed meanwhile.

## Build QA

- [ ] Campaign/ad-group/keyword/ad/asset names and parent links validate; all imports remain paused.
- [ ] Exact/phrase launch keywords only; later broad terms remain held; negatives checked for conflicts.
- [ ] Two meaningfully distinct RSAs per launch ad group; character script passes; every combination is understandable and true.
- [ ] Geo targets/exclusions inspected visually; metro/Northshore overlap controlled; Mississippi excluded.
- [ ] Search Network only; Search Partners/Display expansion off initially; language and schedule approved.
- [ ] Final URLs return correctly on mobile, preserve tracking, and match intent; no placeholder URLs published.

## Approval and rollback

- [ ] Owner approves final copy, assets, landing pages, budget, launch subset, date, operator, and daily caps in writing.
- [ ] Change preview exported; rollback/pause owner and anomaly alerts named.
- [ ] Day-1, day-3, day-7, day-14, and day-30 reviews scheduled around conversion lag and capacity.
