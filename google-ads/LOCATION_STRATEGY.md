# Location Strategy

**Guardrail:** target only owner-approved service areas. The place list is proposed, not proof of coverage or profitability.

## Segments

| Segment | Proposed treatment | Launch gate | Exclusions/safeguards |
|---|---|---|---|
| New Orleans core | Primary metro campaigns; city target plus approved ZIP/city refinement | Serviceability, minimums, landing-page truth, capacity | Exclude unsupported surrounding areas; review location report weekly |
| Metairie / Jefferson Parish | Include in core initially; split only when volume supports decisions | Confirm parish/town coverage | Do not assume all parish communities are served |
| Westbank | Observation-only proposal or separate controlled campaign | Owner confirms travel, minimum, and win economics | Exclude until approved; never infer profitability from clicks |
| Northshore | Separate campaign and budget | Approved towns, travel/minimum rules, local page and proof | Exclude from metro campaigns to prevent overlap |
| Mississippi | Do not launch | Explicit owner approval, license/service/legal review, economics, pages | Exclude Mississippi at account/campaign level until approved |

## Targeting mode

Use the current platform option equivalent to **people in or regularly in the targeted locations**, not presence-or-interest, after confirming the exact UI wording. Exclude people in excluded locations. Do not use radius targeting that unintentionally crosses Lake Pontchartrain or state boundaries without inspecting the map.

## Proposed geo map

- **Core:** New Orleans and Metairie are proposed launch targets, subject to owner confirmation.
- **Jefferson Parish:** add only confirmed municipalities/ZIPs; avoid a blanket parish target until coverage is answered.
- **Northshore hold set:** Mandeville, Covington, Madisonville, and Slidell, each pending approval and economics.
- **Westbank hold set:** owner-defined cities/ZIPs only.
- **Hard exclusions:** all countries except the United States; states outside Louisiana; Mississippi until approved; any town/ZIP repeatedly producing unserviceable leads.

## Overlap control

Metro campaigns exclude approved Northshore targets; Northshore excludes core targets. Brand may span all approved territory but must report geography. Geographic terms do not replace physical-presence targeting. Location insertion is prohibited until combinations and service-area truth are QA’d.

## Review rules

Weekly for the first month, then at least biweekly: user location, matched location, serviceability, qualified rate, estimate rate, win value, travel burden, and spend. Exclude only on sufficient evidence or clear nonserviceability. Do not cut a viable area for one expensive click.
