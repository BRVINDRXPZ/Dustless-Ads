# B2B Minimum Viable Activation

**Status:** Internal, inactive draft. No launch, publication, outreach, account connection, or spend is authorized. Owner approval required.

## Smallest launchable system
Begin with internal list building, then only separately approved, individualized contact to existing contacts, permissioned warm introductions, past contractors, suppliers, remodelers, builders, designers and realtors. No mass cold outreach. Commercial prospects remain held unless vendor/capability gates pass.

**Before contact:** permissioned service-matched proof; verified credentials only as applicable; one-page capability document with no placeholders; current capacity/minimums/geography; owner-approved account and lawful channel. Create a small account list with Account ID, segment, relationship/source, person/role, permission/lawful basis, relevant need, proof match, owner, last/next action, stage, outcome and suppression. Personalize using verified business relevance—never surveillance or invented familiarity.

**Sequence:** owner approves named account → owner/warm introducer makes human contact → log response → one agreed follow-up or restrained approved cadence → goal is a fit conversation/site-walk opportunity, not volume → measure conversations, qualified opportunities, estimates/proposals, wins, revenue/GP when available. Stop on opt-out, wrong contact, no proof/capacity, complaint, legal uncertainty, repeated nonresponse per approved limit, or operational pause.
