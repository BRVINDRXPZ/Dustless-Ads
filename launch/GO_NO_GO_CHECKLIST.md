## Automatic NO-GO

Any NO/blank; broken or duplicate conversion; lead/PII leakage; unstaffed phone/form; unapproved spend, service, geography, claim, copy or asset; missing permission; failed routing; inability to pause; unresolved platform/policy warning; no production backup; capacity stop; owner withdrawal; or material staging/production mismatch. Stop the affected campaign, record the incident, roll back, fix, retest the entire affected path, and obtain renewed approval.
