# Draft Project Permission Workflow

**Status:** Internal launch-control draft; owner completion and written approval required. **No live change, connection, publication, contact, or spend is authorized.** Blank/TBD fields are intentional.

> Process draft for qualified legal review; **not final release language or legal advice**.

1. Assign a non-identifying project ID before capture. 2. Explain each optional use separately (capture, organic publication, location detail, testimonial, company name, paid advertising, case study). 3. Use counsel-reviewed release; no bundled or pre-checked permissions. 4. Customer signs; owner stores evidence outside Git. 5. Permission owner records scope, restrictions, expiry and evidence URI. 6. A second reviewer checks asset, caption, claim and channel before use. 7. Log publication locations. 8. On revocation/expiry, stop new use and follow reviewed takedown procedure. Minors, sensitive locations, visible private information, and drone operations require separate qualified review.
