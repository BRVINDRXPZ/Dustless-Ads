# Google Ads Minimum Viable Launch

**Status:** Internal, inactive draft. No launch, publication, outreach, account connection, or spend is authorized. Owner approval required.

## Exact initial launch
Launch only service campaigns whose individual gates pass. **Eligible set:** Brand Defense, Hardwood Refinishing, Dust-Controlled Sanding, Hardwood Installation. Selected Repair/Restoration remains conditional on owner-approved minimums, proof and page. Commercial, contractor, historic/heart pine, Northshore, stairs, Performance Max, broad match, display and paid social are held.

**Negatives:** load shared employment/jobs/DIY/training/rental/equipment/material-only/free/wholesale and wrong-flooring lists, then service-specific exclusions. Review each term for accidental blocking. **Geography:** owner-approved core locations only, presence setting validated in current UI; exact inclusions/exclusions blank until owner completes register. **Schedule:** only hours with named responder and fallback; blank until approved.

**Budget:** Approved monthly ceiling = `$_____`; approved daily account ceiling = `$_____`; campaign daily allocation = `daily account ceiling × owner-approved campaign %`. Percentages must total 100%; no recommendation presented as a factual optimum. Budget/billing changes require signoff. **Bidding:** controlled current-platform-validated launch method without invented CPCs; do not use value-based automation until qualified outcomes and reconciliation are reliable.

**Conversions:** primary = human-validated qualified form and qualified call only after tracking QA; raw forms/calls/clicks diagnostic. **Calls/forms:** route to named responder; preserve IDs; consent and missed-call process pass.

**Controls:** daily: spend/ceiling, delivery/geography, search terms/negatives, disapprovals, routing, duplicates, lead quality, capacity. First week: reconcile every lead and adjust only with documented reason. First month: assess qualified leads, estimates, wins and economics—not clicks alone. **Scale only** when tracking/reconciliation pass, capacity is available, owner approves, and a repeatable qualified outcome exists. **Pause** for broken tracking/routing/page, unsafe query/geography, unstaffed response, budget breach, claim/policy issue, capacity stop, or owner withdrawal. Written `LAUNCH_APPROVAL_FORM.md` is mandatory.
