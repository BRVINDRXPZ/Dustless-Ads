# Social Minimum Viable Activation

**Status:** Internal, inactive draft. No launch, publication, outreach, account connection, or spend is authorized. Owner approval required.

## Sustainable first month
Use only three franchises: **Can These Floors Be Saved?**, **The Floor Guys Explain**, and **Finish Friday/before-and-after project story**. Target cadence is a proposal: 2 feed videos/posts per week plus one GBP repurpose only when the owner confirms capture/edit/approval/response capacity. Reduce cadence rather than lower proof or permission standards.

**Capture minimum:** project ID; vertical master plus optional horizontal; before/process/after; clean audio; no private data/location leakage; factual scope notes; customer/property/crew/organic/paid permissions separately logged. **Workflow:** capture lead uploads to restricted storage → editor creates one master → fact/claims and permission review → owner written approval → publisher schedules → responder monitors comments/DMs during approved hours → lead logged in CRM. Never answer technical/project-specific questions beyond verified facts.

Weekly review: approved pieces completed, saves/completion/qualified inquiries as diagnostics, lead quality and production burden. **Scale** a franchise only after repeatable production and qualified attention without claim/privacy incidents. **Kill/pause** after a permission/claim issue, unsustainable workload, repeated poor-fit leads, or no useful learning after an owner-defined test window. No paid amplification at MVP.
