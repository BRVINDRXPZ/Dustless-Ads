# Call Tracking Plan

**Vendor-neutral and provisional.** No number or vendor is selected.

- **Calls from ads:** platform forwarding where eligible; preserve campaign attribution; do not count every connection as qualified.
- **Website calls:** dynamic number insertion (DNI) after consent/legal review, with a tested fallback business number and source/session mapping that does not alter visible NAP where inappropriate.
- **Threshold:** 60 seconds is a provisional reporting candidate, not proof of quality. Human review or structured disposition determines `qualified_phone_call`.
- Review connected/missed/repeat calls daily during launch, classify homeowner/contractor/commercial, service, geography, qualification and reason without exporting sensitive call content to analytics.
- Missed calls enter an owner-approved callback queue; no response-time promise exists yet. After-hours uses owner-approved voicemail/routing and sets expectations truthfully.
- Dedupe repeat calls using provider call ID plus normalized caller/lead linkage and a provisional 30-day opportunity window; preserve each call activity while counting one lead/outcome as appropriate.
- Recording/transcription, announcement, consent, retention, access, and Louisiana/other applicable law require legal approval. Default to no recording until approved.
- Test business-hours, after-hours, blocked caller ID, DNI failure, mobile click, desktop display, forwarding, voicemail, attribution expiry, and CRM reconciliation.
