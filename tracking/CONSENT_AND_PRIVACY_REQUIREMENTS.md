# Consent and Privacy Requirements

This is an operational draft, not legal advice. Owner and qualified counsel must approve applicable privacy, electronic-contact, cookie/tag, call recording, retention, deletion, and vendor terms.

Use data minimization, stated purpose, least privilege, encryption in transit/at rest, access logs, vendor agreements, retention limits, deletion/correction workflow, incident procedure, and separate production/test data. Do not expose personal data in URLs, analytics, ad parameters, logs beyond necessity, or this repository.

Contact consent must be affirmative, unbundled where law/purpose requires, versioned, timestamped server-side, and linked to the exact disclosed channels. Essential form processing must be distinguished from advertising measurement. Tags/DNI behavior must respect the approved consent regime. No prerecorded/automated marketing consent is inferred from a general estimate request.
