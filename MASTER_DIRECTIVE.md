## Mission

Build NOLA Dustless Hardwood Floors into the dominant hardwood-flooring brand in the Greater New Orleans market.

The desired market position is:

When a homeowner, contractor, architect, designer, property manager, hotel operator, restaurant owner, realtor, or commercial developer thinks about hardwood flooring, NOLA Dustless should be one of the first companies that enters their mind.

## Marketing principles

- Dominate high-intent demand before chasing broad awareness.
- Build a recognizable local brand rather than a generic contractor identity.
- Use real project proof.
- Make before-and-after documentation a core business process.
- Treat Google Search and Google Maps as primary acquisition channels.
- Use social media to create familiarity, trust, authority, and repeated exposure.
- Build contractor relationships as a separate acquisition channel.
- Measure qualified leads, estimates, proposals, wins, revenue, and gross profit.
- Do not judge success only by clicks, followers, impressions, or cheap leads.
- Build systems that improve after every project.
- Avoid unnecessary discounting.
- Avoid unsupported “best,” “number one,” or exclusivity claims.
- Never use fake urgency or fake scarcity.
