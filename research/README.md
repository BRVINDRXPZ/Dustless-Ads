# Research Workspace

Store Phase 2 research briefs, cited evidence, methods, and findings.

Phase 2B controls:

- `EVIDENCE_REGISTER.csv` is the central claim-to-source ledger.
- `COMPETITOR_VALIDATION_QUEUE.csv` controls validation order and completeness.
- The five `*_TEMPLATE.csv` files preserve observation context by surface.
- `OWNER_RESEARCH_INSTRUCTIONS.md` defines collection, naming, privacy, and handoff.
- `PHASE_2_EVIDENCE_GAPS.md` records what remains provisional and the exit criteria.

Candidate companies, rankings, reviews, Search/Maps visibility, ads, social activity, services, territory, offers, and positioning remain unverified unless linked to dated evidence. Never interpret “not observed” as absence.
