# Competitive Claims to Verify

This is a verification queue, not an accusation that any named competitor makes a false claim. Live pages were inaccessible on 2026-07-20, so no competitor-specific claim is recorded as observed.

| Claim class | Examples to capture verbatim in future (short excerpt only) | Verification needed | NOLA Dustless rule |
|---|---|---|---|
| Dust | dust-free, dustless, 99% dust capture | Equipment, process, test basis, exclusions | Prefer precise “dust-controlled” language after owner verification |
| Superiority | best, #1, leading, most trusted | Independent award/ranking methodology and date | Do not use without substantiation |
| Experience | years in business, generations, project count | Formation/history and project records | Use only verified exact facts |
| Credentials | licensed, insured, bonded, certified | Current license/COI/certification; scope and expiry | Owner-approved wording only |
| Reviews | five-star, hundreds of reviews | Platform, current count/rating/date | Cite platform/date accurately; do not aggregate deceptively |
| Historic expertise | heart pine, antique pine, preservation specialist | Training and documented relevant projects | Require species/project evidence |
| Commercial capability | hotel, restaurant, church, major commercial | Permissioned project records/references | No named client/property without permission |
| Warranty | lifetime, satisfaction guaranteed | Written terms, exclusions, responsible party | Publish only approved written terms |
| Finish performance | most durable, commercial grade, low-VOC | Product technical data and use conditions | Attribute exact manufacturer data; avoid universal claims |
| Speed | one-day, same-day, rapid turnaround | Defined scope and historical support | Quote scope-specific schedule, not a blanket promise |
| Price/offer | lowest price, free estimate, financing | Current policy and terms | Owner approval; no fake urgency |
| Repair quality | seamless, invisible, perfect match | Outcome variability and documented cases | Explain matching limits |
| Service area | all Greater New Orleans/Northshore | Operational coverage and minimums | List only approved areas |

## Future capture fields

For each observed claim record: company, exact page/profile URL, date/time, short exact wording, claim class, placement, supporting evidence shown, independent verification status, screenshot location, and implication. Do not copy long competitor text or imagery.

## Claims requiring owner confirmation now

1. Licensed/insured wording and entities covered.
2. Dust-control equipment, setup, expected residual dust, and exclusions.
3. Historic, antique-pine, and heart-pine experience.
4. Commercial, hospitality, church, and institutional project history.
5. Installation methods actually offered and scope limitations.
6. Finish systems, warranties, cure guidance, and certifications.
7. Service territory, response expectations, and Northshore minimums.
8. Named projects, customers, reviews, and media usage rights.
