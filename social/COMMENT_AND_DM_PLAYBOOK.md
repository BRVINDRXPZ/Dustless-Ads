# Comment and DM Playbook
> **Internal draft only.** Nothing is approved for publication, outreach, account connection, or spend. Brackets require verified facts and permissions.

## Rules
Acknowledge publicly when appropriate, then move customer/project facts to the approved private form/phone/DM. Never request sensitive information in comments; never diagnose, quote, promise availability, admit liability, attack, or delete legitimate criticism. Record source/content ID and consent before CRM routing.

## “How much?”
**Public/DM framework:** Pricing depends on area, condition, repairs, species, stairs, furniture/access, finish, occupancy and matching. We don’t want to guess publicly. Please use [approved estimate path] with location, approximate area and photos.
**Escalate:** Any safety, injury, legal, threat, active complaint, privacy, payment, or media issue to the owner immediately.
