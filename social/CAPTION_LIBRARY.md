## Keyword and hashtag system
Write for humans first: naturally include the verified service, decision, safe city/metro level, material (only when verified), and project type. Use 0–5 relevant tags from an approved bank such as `#HardwoodFloors`, `#FloorRefinishing`, `#NewOrleansHomes`, `#NOLAHomes`, `#NorthshoreLA` only when the geography/service is true. Hashtags are labels, not ranking guarantees. Never attach a neighborhood merely for reach.
