# Community Engagement Playbook
> **Internal draft only.** No publication, outreach, live connection, spend, or asset use is authorized. Verify claims and record permissions before use.

## Daily (only while accounts are authorized and staffed)
Two owner-approved response windows: triage comments, DMs, Story replies, mentions, lead questions, complaints, spam, and unsafe requests. Respond specifically, not with fake comments. Like/reply to relevant local/trade posts only when there is a genuine contribution; no engagement pods or quota spam.

## Weekly
- Review unanswered threads, saved FAQs, response time baseline, inquiry quality, contractor/realtor/designer/local-business/supplier/hospitality/creator conversations.
- Leave a small number of substantive professional comments where NOLA Dustless has real context; never pitch in unrelated threads.
- Convert recurring questions into drafts without exposing the asker.
- Reconcile inquiries to approved CRM source fields; review blocks, hides, deletions, and escalations.

## Triage
| Type | Action |
|---|---|
| Genuine question/DIY | Give decision factors, not step-by-step hazardous instruction; route project specifics privately |
| Job inquiry | Collect minimum qualification only in approved private path; consent/privacy rules apply |
| B2B/local relationship | Acknowledge; log category; no outbound follow-up without approval |
| Positive | Thank specifically without soliciting review or fabricating relationship |
| Negative/price criticism | Stay calm; clarify general principle; do not argue or disclose scope |
| Complaint | Acknowledge, capture link/time, move private, notify owner immediately; no admission/denial before facts |
| Employment | Direct to approved employment channel if one exists; do not promise openings |
| Spam/scam | Capture if needed, then hide/block/report under policy |
| Unsafe advice | Decline remote instruction; recommend stopping work and appropriate site assessment/emergency help |
