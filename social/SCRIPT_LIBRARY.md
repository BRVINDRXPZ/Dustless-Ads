# Script Library

> **Status:** Internal production system—drafts only. Nothing here authorizes publishing, account connection, outreach, spend, or use of an asset without recorded permission and claim review.

## Operating guardrails
- Use real, accurately labeled project media only. AI/synthetic imagery is never project proof.
- A project, customer, testimonial, precise location, property exterior, crew member, product claim, credential, result, schedule, or price appears only after its specific fact and permission fields are cleared.
- Say “dust-controlled” only after process wording is approved; never promise dust-free conditions.
- Move addresses, scope details, availability, budgets, complaints, and customer information to the approved private lead path.
- Draft, review, and schedule are separate states. Only the owner-designated publisher may publish after final approval.

## Production use
Bracketed facts are placeholders, never fill them by inference. Short scripts are spoken naturally; crews may paraphrase without changing verified meaning.
## SCR-001 — Surface Wear Or Deeper Damage
- **Franchise / audience / length:** The Floor Guys Explain; Homeowner; 25–45 seconds.
- **Visual hook:** Open on a real close-up or scope artifact showing **[surface wear or deeper damage]**, then a matching wide shot.
- **Spoken hook:** “Before we decide what this floor needs, here’s the surface wear or deeper damage question we have to answer.”
- **Main talking points:** (1) state the observed condition only; (2) explain the approved decision factors in plain language; (3) show the relevant process or document; (4) state what cannot be concluded remotely; (5) show the verified outcome or next decision.
- **B-roll:** Matched before angle; marked condition; protection/setup; safe process detail; transition/edge; matched after only if complete; approved scope document with private data hidden.
- **On-screen text:** “Surface Wear Or Deeper Damage”; “Condition → options → verified next step”; subtitles; final CTA.
- **Proof required:** Asset permission; project scope; observed condition; process/product/finish facts; after-state if used; species/history/credential review where applicable.
- **Disclaimer:** “A site assessment and project details determine the appropriate scope; this video is educational, not a quote or guarantee.”
- **CTA:** “Send the floor type, approximate area, location, timing, and clear photos through the approved estimate path.”
- **Repurposing:** 9:16 short; 4:5 carousel from five frames; Story Q&A; GBP only when locally relevant and cleared; clean paid-ready cut only after paid approval.
