# Project Content Capture System
> **Internal draft only.** No publication, outreach, live connection, spend, or asset use is authorized. Verify claims and record permissions before use.

## Qualification and stop gate
Content lead marks a project **Qualified / Hold / No capture**. Before recording people, interiors, exterior, neighborhood, name, or location, record owner/customer/property/crew permissions separately. No release means operational documentation remains restricted and cannot enter marketing drafts.

## Before work checklist
Permissioned exterior/neighborhood context; room-wide views; condition/damage close-ups; transitions; stairs; existing color/sheen/repairs; customer objective stated in approved terms; scope; optional consented crew introduction. Photograph a camera-position marker and record lens/height/direction.

## During work checklist
Protection; equipment setup; sanding; edging; vacuuming; repairs; board replacement; labeled stain samples; finish preparation/application; approved contractor coordination; approved commercial phasing. Never interrupt safe work or reveal access/security/client documents.

## After work checklist
Exact matched camera position; wide and low-angle reveal; natural-light view; details; transitions; stairs; accurate sheen; optional crew walkthrough; reaction only with explicit testimonial/media permission; maintenance instructions only from approved system/product sources.

## Capture standard
- **Orientation:** primary 9:16 vertical, plus locked 16:9 wides for hero/long-form; stills with crop room.
- **Resolution/frame rate:** 4K where storage permits; 24/30fps normal motion; 60fps only for intentional slow motion. Never interpolate fake detail.
- **Lighting/color:** clean lens; avoid mixed lighting where possible; lock white balance/exposure for matched shots; include neutral card on color-critical work; no filter that alters floor color.
- **Movement/stability:** tripod for matches; slow gimbal/handheld moves; no whip-pan template that hides condition.
- **Audio:** lav/shotgun for speech; room tone and isolated equipment takes; monitor peaks; do not record private conversation.

## File and folder standard
`P-[internal-project-id]/01_ADMIN/`, `02_BEFORE/`, `03_DURING/`, `04_AFTER/`, `05_AUDIO/`, `06_SELECTS/`, `07_EDITS/`, `08_EXPORTS/`.
Filename: `P-ID_YYYYMMDD_STAGE_ROOM_SUBJECT_ORIENT_TAKE_CREATOR.ext`; never put customer name/address in filename. Maintain working copy plus separate backup; access restricted by role. Confirm checksum/spot playback before clearing camera cards.

## Metadata and asset states
Record service, audience, residential/commercial, safe location level, capture date, stage, shot ID, source creator, floor/species as unverified or verified, product/finish, project facts, permissions by use, claim reviewer, expiration/withdrawal, and derivatives. States: `Restricted source → Logged → Permission pending → Fact review → Edit cleared → Approval pending → Approved/scheduled → Published → Archived/withdrawn`. No derivative may outrun its parent rights.

## Privacy and matching QA
Strip GPS; exclude mail, plates, family photos, alarm/access details, minors, valuables, exact address, neighboring properties, and confidential plans. Use location level `None / metro / city / neighborhood / named property`; select the least specific approved level. Before/after must match lens, height, direction, light as reasonably possible and must label different lighting/time when material. A revocation or correction freezes scheduling and triggers derivative search.
