# Creative Testing Plan
> **Internal draft only.** Nothing is approved for publication, outreach, account connection, or spend. Brackets require verified facts and permissions.

## Method
One variable per paired test; same audience, platform, subject, distribution status and comparable window. “Minimum sample” means **at least 6 comparable posts per cell and enough account-level observations for directional review**; it is not statistical proof. If reach differs materially, compare rate metrics and do not declare a winner. Adopt only when primary and business metrics improve directionally across at least two projects with no guardrail decline; otherwise retain/retest. Paid tests require a separate power/budget plan and approval.
