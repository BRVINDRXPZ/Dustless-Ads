# CTA Library
> **Internal draft only.** No publication, outreach, live connection, spend, or asset use is authorized. Verify claims and record permissions before use.

## Awareness
- Save this condition checklist for your project conversation.
- Share this with the person coordinating the renovation.
- Follow for real hardwood assessments, process, and reveals.
## Consideration
- Compare your floor against these factors; do not diagnose from one photo.
- Send clear wide and detail photos through the approved estimate form.
- Ask which scope—repair, refinish, restore, or install—fits after assessment.
## Decision
- Request an assessment with floor type, approximate area, location, timing, and access constraints.
- Contractors: send plans, area, substrate, material, schedule, access, and decision date.
- Commercial teams: request a scope review with use, phasing, operating hours, and procurement requirements.
## GBP/local
- View the relevant service page for process, fit, and next steps.
- Call during approved hours or use the estimate form; never promise response timing unless confirmed.
## Disallowed CTA patterns
No fake deadlines, “last spots,” guaranteed savings/results, diagnosis promises, comment bait, engagement pods, or pressure to expose private project facts publicly.
