# Posting Workflow
> **Internal draft only.** No publication, outreach, live connection, spend, or asset use is authorized. Verify claims and record permissions before use.

1. **Monday inventory:** choose at most three core ideas from cleared sources; name audience, funnel job, owner, derivative plan.
2. **Brief:** assign content/script/shot IDs, claims, permission, CTA, required destination, due dates.
3. **Produce:** capture to standard; ingest/backup/register before formatting cards.
4. **Edit:** clean master first; platform variants second; do not bake unlicensed music into master.
5. **Review:** technical fact owner → privacy/rights → brand/copy → operational capacity → owner final.
6. **Schedule packet:** platform, date/time test, caption, alt text, cover, tags, link/UTM proposal, community monitor, rollback file. Scheduling is not approval.
7. **Publisher preflight:** recheck current permission/claim/link/feature/capacity; publish only under separate authorization.
8. **Monitor:** acknowledge normal questions, route leads privately, screenshot/escalate complaints, never delete criticism merely for being negative.
9. **Measure:** 24h/7d/28d where data exists; record business outcomes after attribution reconciliation.
10. **Archive/learn:** register derivatives, retain master and approval, note test; freeze withdrawn assets.

**Status board:** Backlog / Briefed / Capture pending / Editing / Fact review / Permission hold / Owner review / Approved-not-authorized / Scheduled-authorized / Published / Reuse / Archived / Withdrawn.
