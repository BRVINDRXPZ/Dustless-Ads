# Eating With Cait and Lagniappe Social Co. Plan
> **Planning only.** No publication, outreach, live system connection, campaign creation, spend, or use of uncleared proof is authorized.

## Strategic boundary
Use these owner-identified relationships as potential production/local-business bridges—not as evidence of affiliation, endorsement, access, performance, or permission. Keep NOLA Dustless a hardwood brand; no random food posts.

## Appropriate formats
- Lagniappe: production-system audit, shot-day planning, editing templates, creator-rights workflow, local cultural review.
- Eating With Cait: only permissioned renovation/opening stories where flooring is genuinely relevant; hospitality/restaurant-owner introduction with explicit consent; local-business craft conversation; earned-reach collaboration with clear disclosure and separate brand roles.
- Joint: verified commercial case study; “what operators should plan before floor work” conversation; behind-the-scenes production learning—not a disguised endorsement.

## Inappropriate formats
Unrelated restaurant post with flooring pitch; surprise filming; implied customer/partner status; free-work-for-positive-coverage exchange; creator testimonial without experience; access to private lists; undisclosed compensation/referrals; copyrighted restaurant/customer footage; sharing customer data; guaranteed reach/leads.

## Commercial-introduction process
Owner identifies allowed category → conflict check → obtain both sides’ permission to introduce → send minimal context only in future authorized work → NOLA qualifies scope directly → record source/consent/status → separate creator from procurement/estimate decision → disclose any financial interest. No introduction occurs under this plan.

## Role/rights matrix
NOLA owns technical accuracy, scope, customer permission and final approval. Production partner may advise/capture/edit only under written scope and data rules. Creator controls their likeness/channel; property/customer controls location/project permissions. Agree raw/master/derivative/paid usage, credit, term, geography, music, disclosure, correction and withdrawal before capture.

## Tracking and safeguards
Partnership ID, introduction source/date/consent, category, deliverables, costs (owner-approved only), rights, disclosures, assisted inquiry, qualification, estimate/win only from CRM, assets/reuse, conflicts and notes. Separate brand accounts, visuals, CTAs, email lists, analytics and invoices. Recuse any party with undisclosed economic interest.

## Success measures
Permissioned useful assets, qualified commercial introductions, assisted opportunities, reusable production learning, relevant audience quality, brand-safety exceptions (target zero), and partner burden—not raw reach alone.
