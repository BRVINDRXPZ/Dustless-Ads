## Non-negotiable start gates
1. Owner-approved budget/stop rules and separate written launch approval.
2. Current official Meta policy/UI validation; Business Manager ownership/2FA/roles.
3. Pixel and server-side/conversion tracking design, consent/legal review, test events, dedupe, attribution and offline CRM outcomes.
4. Approved landing pages or native lead form, privacy notice, truthful CTA, accessible flow, spam handling and lead-response SLA with capacity.
5. At least three distinct cleared concepts with aspect/copy variations; rights survive paid use; no platform-watermarked/unlicensed audio.
6. Proof register: customer/project/location/testimonial/result permissions and withdrawal procedure.
7. Geographic/service eligibility; customer/employee/current-lead and other approved exclusions; sensitive category safeguards.
8. CRM fields for campaign/content/creative, disposition, qualified lead, estimate, win, revenue/value definition and corrections.
9. QA: test lead end-to-end, mobile, routing, notifications, consent record, source IDs, suppression, accessibility and rollback.

## Creative readiness packet
Clean 9:16, 4:5, 1:1 masters; 6/15/30/45s cuts; textless versions; caption/SRT; cover; licensed audio proof; source asset lineage; claims sheet; three hooks; two CTAs; landing match; approved safe-area/location. Store “paid use allowed” separately from organic permission.

## Hold conditions
No launch with unknown response ownership, broken conversion, insufficient capacity, unclear consent, absent exclusions, unapproved budget, unsupported offer/prices/testimonial, weak proof volume, or unresolved privacy/complaint risk.
