# Reporting Cadence

> **Planning specification — not connected or activated.** No live accounts, credentials, customer data, or invented business performance. Any prototype values are synthetic demonstration data.

## Purpose

Defines review rituals and accountable outputs.

## Daily

New leads; missed calls; response time; broken forms; urgent commercial opportunities; spend anomalies. Owner: sales/marketing operator. Output: exception queue, no strategy changes from one day.

## Weekly

Qualified leads; estimates; proposals; wins; lead quality; Ads search terms; organic visibility; content; B2B pipeline; stalls; data quality. Output: actions, owners, due dates.

## Monthly

Revenue and gross profit by source; CAC; close rate; campaign economics; local SEO; reviews; social assists; contractor pipeline; lost reasons; constraints. Finance signs reconciliation before economics decisions.

## Quarterly

Positioning, service/geography mix, allocation, CRM fitness, automation, content franchises, contractor accounts, commercial expansion, hiring/capacity. Owner approves changes.

## Meeting rule

Start with data-quality exceptions and Level 1; document decision/evidence. Vanity metrics cannot close an action.
