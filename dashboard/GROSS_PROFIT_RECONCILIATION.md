# Gross Profit Reconciliation

> **Planning specification — not connected or activated.** No live accounts, credentials, customer data, or invented business performance. Any prototype values are synthetic demonstration data.

## Purpose

Prevents revenue proxies from masquerading as profitability.

## Definitions

Actual GP = recognized revenue − approved attributable direct job costs under the owner/accountant-approved cost policy. Booked/estimated GP is separately labeled. Overhead contribution is not called gross profit unless accounting policy says so.

## Procedure

Start with reconciled project revenue; import cost ledger by project/cost category; validate credits/refunds/change orders; identify missing/unposted costs and completion state; calculate only eligible projects; compare estimate vs actual; finance approves and locks period.

## Exceptions

GP missing when costs incomplete; GP > revenue is invalid unless documented extraordinary credit and finance approval; negative GP is retained, not hidden; shared costs use documented allocation; margin variance creates human review; no invented cost assumptions.

## Attribution

Attach actual GP to the project’s governed primary source only after reconciliation; assists remain non-additive. Report GP coverage and pending amount.
