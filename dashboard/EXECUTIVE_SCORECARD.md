# Executive Scorecard

> **Planning specification — not connected or activated.** No live accounts, credentials, customer data, or invented business performance. Any prototype values are synthetic demonstration data.

## Purpose

Weekly/monthly specification; no current values.

## Card set

Marketing spend; Total leads; Qualified leads; Estimate requests; Estimates scheduled; Estimates completed; Proposals sent; Jobs won; Revenue booked; Gross profit booked; CPL; cost per qualified lead; cost per scheduled estimate; CAC; ROAS; gross-profit ROAS; close rate; average job value; average gross profit/job; pipeline value; weighted pipeline; median lead-response time; missed-call rate; referral contribution; repeat-account revenue.

## Display contract

Each card shows definition, reporting period, current value or **Unavailable—source not integrated**, prior comparable period only when real, freshness, coverage, and exception flag. Currency uses USD; ratios show numerator/denominator.

## Cuts

Source, service, geography, segment, owner. Suppress or flag misleading small samples rather than imply certainty.

## Decision table

Review Level 1 first. For each source record **Scale / Fix / Hold / Stop**, evidence, capacity check, owner, due date. Diagnostic improvement alone cannot justify scaling.
