# Designer And Architect Value Proposition

> **Internal draft — owner approval required.** This is an operating specification, not authorization to contact, publish, connect a CRM, promise capacity, or make a public claim. Bracketed fields and `TBD—owner verification required` are not public copy. Use only public business contact data or permissioned records.

## Position
Translate design intent into a defined hardwood scope through existing-condition documentation, repair/matching limitations, sample/finish decision records, constructability questions, sequencing and change visibility.
## Collaboration
Ask early about species/profile/source, transitions, stairs, sheen/color, substrate/moisture, samples/mockups, approvals, protection, maintenance and who owns each decision. Never guarantee an exact match. Protect authorship, project confidentiality and photo permissions.
