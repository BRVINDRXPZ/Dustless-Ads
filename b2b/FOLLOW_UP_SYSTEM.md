# Follow Up System

> **Internal draft — owner approval required.** This is an operating specification, not authorization to contact, publish, connect a CRM, promise capacity, or make a public claim. Bracketed fields and `TBD—owner verification required` are not public copy. Use only public business contact data or permissioned records.

## Cadences
### No response
- **Cadence:** Days 1, 7, 18 then Dormant. **Guardrail:** Three relevant touches maximum.
- Log last activity, response, next action/date, owner, stage, stop/permission status and evidence shared.
### Initial conversation
- **Cadence:** Recap same/next business day; next agreed date. **Guardrail:** Never manufacture a reason.
- Log last activity, response, next action/date, owner, stage, stop/permission status and evidence shared.
### Plans promised
- **Cadence:** Agreed date + 2 business days; one close-loop. **Guardrail:** Do not hold capacity.
- Log last activity, response, next action/date, owner, stage, stop/permission status and evidence shared.
### Bid submitted
- **Cadence:** Receipt check 1–2 business days; decision-date follow-up; one spaced close-loop. **Guardrail:** Respect procurement instructions.
- Log last activity, response, next action/date, owner, stage, stop/permission status and evidence shared.
### Waiting on decision
- **Cadence:** At stated decision date, then mutually agreed. **Guardrail:** No daily chasing.
- Log last activity, response, next action/date, owner, stage, stop/permission status and evidence shared.
### Project delayed
- **Cadence:** At recorded trigger/date, usually 30–90 days. **Guardrail:** Keep Deferred, value assumption flagged.
- Log last activity, response, next action/date, owner, stage, stop/permission status and evidence shared.
### Lost bid
- **Cadence:** Thank/feedback once; re-engage only on permission/date. **Guardrail:** No argument.
- Log last activity, response, next action/date, owner, stage, stop/permission status and evidence shared.
### Won project
- **Cadence:** Mobilization milestones per contract. **Guardrail:** Operational messages replace selling.
- Log last activity, response, next action/date, owner, stage, stop/permission status and evidence shared.
### Post-project
- **Cadence:** Closeout; 7–14 day check; relationship review by permission. **Guardrail:** Do not solicit public proof without rights.
- Log last activity, response, next action/date, owner, stage, stop/permission status and evidence shared.
### Dormant account
- **Cadence:** Quarterly or event-trigger only when relevant/allowed. **Guardrail:** Stop if no consent/relevance.
- Log last activity, response, next action/date, owner, stage, stop/permission status and evidence shared.
### Strategic partner
- **Cadence:** Active-project cadence plus quarterly account review. **Guardrail:** Capacity never promised.
- Log last activity, response, next action/date, owner, stage, stop/permission status and evidence shared.
### Referral partner
- **Cadence:** Thank each accepted referral; quarterly fit review if welcomed. **Guardrail:** No undisclosed quid pro quo.
- Log last activity, response, next action/date, owner, stage, stop/permission status and evidence shared.

## Service standard
Every follow-up must add clarity, answer a question, confirm a decision date or close the loop. Suppress opted-out/no-fit records. Weekly overdue report; manager approves exceptions. Live-project safety/contract communication follows project requirements, not marketing cadence.
