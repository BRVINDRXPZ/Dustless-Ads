# Hospitality Value Proposition

> **Internal draft — owner approval required.** This is an operating specification, not authorization to contact, publish, connect a CRM, promise capacity, or make a public claim. Bracketed fields and `TBD—owner verification required` are not public copy. Use only public business contact data or permissioned records.

## Position
Planning-first hardwood support for suitable hospitality and restaurant environments where occupancy, guest experience, operating windows, noise/odor/dust controls, traffic and cure/return-to-service constraints matter.
## Discovery
Map open/closed zones, peak periods, egress, deliveries, protection, ventilation/HVAC, adjacent operations, finish requirements, security, cleanup, handoff and contingency. Offer a walkthrough—not an after-hours or downtime promise.
## Proof gate
Do not use this externally until relevant projects, methods, finish/product facts, vendor readiness, references, permissions and capacity are verified.
