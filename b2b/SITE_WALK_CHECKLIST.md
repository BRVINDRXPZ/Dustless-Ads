# Site Walk Checklist

> **Internal draft — owner approval required.** This is an operating specification, not authorization to contact, publish, connect a CRM, promise capacity, or make a public claim. Bracketed fields and `TBD—owner verification required` are not public copy. Use only public business contact data or permissioned records.

## Before
Confirm appointment/authorized attendees, address, scope/documents, privacy/photo permission, PPE/site rules and measurement tools. A walkthrough does not reserve capacity or guarantee feasibility.
## On site
- [ ] access/parking/loading/security
- [ ] existing flooring and room-by-room scope
- [ ] species/profile uncertainty and matching limits
- [ ] prior sanding/remaining wear layer indicators
- [ ] repairs/board sources
- [ ] moisture observations and testing responsibility
- [ ] subfloor type/condition/flatness
- [ ] furniture/appliances/personal property
- [ ] protection and adjacent finishes
- [ ] stairs/risers/rails
- [ ] transitions/thresholds/baseboards
- [ ] HVAC/environment/ventilation
- [ ] power/electrical/water
- [ ] other trades and sequencing
- [ ] occupancy, people/pets/guest/resident interface
- [ ] commercial traffic/egress
- [ ] phasing/schedule/event constraints
- [ ] material delivery/storage/acclimation
- [ ] debris/disposal/cleanup
- [ ] safety/PPE/site rules
- [ ] permissioned overview/detail photos
- [ ] measurements and basis
- [ ] open questions/decision owners
- [ ] customer/contractor expectations and non-guarantees
## Close
Read back gaps, decisions, exclusions and next step. Log photos by project ID without customer-sensitive data; record who supplied facts. Issue recap with date, document basis, conditions not visible, RFIs, site-readiness owners and bid/no-bid decision.
