# Referral Partner System

> **Internal draft — owner approval required.** This is an operating specification, not authorization to contact, publish, connect a CRM, promise capacity, or make a public claim. Bracketed fields and `TBD—owner verification required` are not public copy. Use only public business contact data or permissioned records.

## Partner contract with the process
Define each partner’s good-fit services/geography/timing/minimums (all owner-TBD), excluded work, handoff permission, customer-consent language, response owner, attribution window/rules, status updates allowed, conflicts and disclosures. Never promise reciprocal leads.
## Handoff
Partner asks prospect permission → sends minimum information through approved channel → NOLA records source and consent → qualifies normally → acknowledges without exposing customer details → communicates only permitted outcome → thanks partner. No fee, gift or co-marketing without owner/legal approval and transparent disclosure.
## Quality review
Quarterly: accepted/declined reasons, qualified leads, estimates, wins, attributed revenue/GP, complaints, consent failures and concentration. Pause on privacy breach, misleading representation, pressure, poor fit or undisclosed consideration.
