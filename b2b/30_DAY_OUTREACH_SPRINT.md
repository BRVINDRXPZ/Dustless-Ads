## Daily discipline
No contact from this plan. Before any future approved contact: account relevance, public/permissioned role, suppression check, tailored message, approved proof, one CTA, capacity check and CRM/activity fields. Friday: reconcile no-response, qualified, opportunity, bid, loss, revenue/GP and capacity—never fabricate blanks.
