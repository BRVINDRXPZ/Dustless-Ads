# Warm Introduction Templates

> **Internal draft — owner approval required.** This is an operating specification, not authorization to contact, publish, connect a CRM, promise capacity, or make a public claim. Bracketed fields and `TBD—owner verification required` are not public copy. Use only public business contact data or permissioned records.

## Ask an introducer
“Would you be comfortable introducing us to [role/company] only if you believe the fit is real? Please do not imply credentials, capacity or an endorsement. Suggested language: ‘[Name], meet [Name] at NOLA Dustless. They are evaluating suitable hardwood partnerships and asked to learn how you handle [specific context]. I’ll let you decide whether a short conversation is useful.’”
## Accept an introduction
“Thank you, [Introducer]. [Recipient], pleased to meet you. [One relevant context sentence.] Could we spend 15 minutes determining whether there is any fit? If not, no follow-up pressure.”
## Decline
“Thank you for thinking of us. Based on [geography/scope/schedule/requirement], we should not represent ourselves as the right fit. We will not refer an alternative unless qualified and permissioned.”
Log introducer permission, any disclosure/compensation, source and outcome.
