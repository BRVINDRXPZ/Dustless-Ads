# Reactivation Campaign

> **Internal draft — owner approval required.** This is an operating specification, not authorization to contact, publish, connect a CRM, promise capacity, or make a public claim. Bracketed fields and `TBD—owner verification required` are not public copy. Use only public business contact data or permissioned records.

## Eligible records
Past relationship is verified, business context is lawful, no opt-out/no-fit conflict, and there is a specific reason to reconnect. Exclude guessed/imported personal data.
## Three-step manual cycle
1. Audit record, prior scope/outcome, permission and unresolved issue. 2. Owner-approved personal note: acknowledge real history, ask what changed, offer a fit review—not a promotion. 3. One follow-up in 7–10 days; close at day 21. Immediate stop on reply/opt-out.
## Prioritization
Former satisfied counterpart with permission; past bidder with constructive fit; supplier/trade relation; dormant account. Do not imply prior client/project facts publicly. Measure reactivated conversations, qualified opportunities, revenue/GP only after reconciliation, opt-outs and reasons—not sends alone.
