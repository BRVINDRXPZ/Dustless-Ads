# 90 Day B2B Roadmap

> **Internal draft — owner approval required.** This is an operating specification, not authorization to contact, publish, connect a CRM, promise capacity, or make a public claim. Bracketed fields and `TBD—owner verification required` are not public copy. Use only public business contact data or permissioned records.

## Days 1–30 — foundation
Proof/vendor/capacity audit; approve segmentation/scoring; configure offline registers; identify permissioned warm network; draft but do not publish capability statements; train bid/site-walk process; create owner-approved shortlist and dry-run messages; begin only after separate outreach approval.
**Exit:** verified proof matrix, capacity/bid rules, secure document owners, clean sample records, approvals and weekly review operating.
## Days 31–60 — controlled learning
Expand only from observed fit; hold contractor meetings/site walks with approval; seek supplier relationships without promises; create truthful case drafts from permissioned work; analyze bid gaps/losses and commercial readiness.
**Exit:** account/opportunity hygiene, first buyer feedback, documented loss lessons, no overdue high-risk gaps.
## Days 61–90 — selective expansion
Evaluate strategic accounts; admit hospitality/property/commercial GC targets only through proof/vendor/capacity gates; run permissioned reactivation/referral/account expansion; reconcile revenue and GP.
**Exit:** owner decision by segment—scale, continue learning, hold, or stop.
## Weekly controls
Mon capacity and next actions; Wed bid/risk review; Fri pipeline/revenue/GP and lessons. Proposed operational targets are set weekly only after capacity and baseline review; none are facts or commitments.
