# Account Management System

> **Internal draft — owner approval required.** This is an operating specification, not authorization to contact, publish, connect a CRM, promise capacity, or make a public claim. Bracketed fields and `TBD—owner verification required` are not public copy. Use only public business contact data or permissioned records.

## Account plan
Record account hypothesis, roles, relationship map, buying process, active/latent opportunities, proof needs, contract/payment norms, preferences, service/geography fit, concentration risk, health (Red/Amber/Green with evidence), growth thesis and next 90-day action.
## Meeting rhythms
After each project: safety/quality/scope/schedule/change/communication/closeout review. Monthly internal review of active A accounts. Quarterly external review only if welcomed: what worked, gaps, forecasted suitable needs (not booked revenue), vendor updates, capacity discussion and introductions.
## Expansion
Earn repeat scope before cross-sell; propose only relevant service/site/team. Re-qualify every project. Track account revenue and GP from reconciled records, referrals, open issues, DSO/payment signals, bid hit rate, repeat cadence and concentration. Downgrade/pause for payment, contract, conduct, scope, capacity or reputational risk.
