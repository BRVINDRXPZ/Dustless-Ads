# Commercial Intake System

> **Internal draft — owner approval required.** This is an operating specification, not authorization to contact, publish, connect a CRM, promise capacity, or make a public claim. Bracketed fields and `TBD—owner verification required` are not public copy. Use only public business contact data or permissioned records.

## Two-gate intake
**Account/vendor gate:** legal contracting party, billing/PO system, contract form, payment/retainage, COI/additional insured, licenses, safety, badging/background, tax status, lien waivers, bonding only if applicable, references and required portals. Mark Available / Missing / Not applicable / Review required; never imply readiness.
**Project gate:** use bid intake plus occupancy, public/guest/resident interface, phasing, downtime/return-to-service expectations, egress, security, deliveries, noise/odor/dust constraints, HVAC, protection, daily turnover, product/submittals/SDS, closeout and facilities contact.
## Decision
Green: requirements supportable and scope/schedule/payment/capacity approved. Amber: named gaps with owner/date. Red: unsupported qualification, unacceptable contract/payment/safety, unclear occupied-work controls, or capacity conflict. Only Green may proceed; Amber cannot be represented as ready.
