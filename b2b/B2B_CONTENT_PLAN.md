## Funnel mapping
Awareness: neutral checklists. Qualification: intake/site-walk tools. Evaluation: permissioned cases/reference offer. Bid: scope/RFI/process samples. Delivery/expansion: closeout lessons and account review. Measure qualified conversations, assisted opportunities and repeat/referral outcomes—not reach alone.
