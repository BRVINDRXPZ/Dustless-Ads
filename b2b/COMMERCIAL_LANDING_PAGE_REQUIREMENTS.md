# Commercial Landing Page Requirements

> **Internal draft — owner approval required.** This is an operating specification, not authorization to contact, publish, connect a CRM, promise capacity, or make a public claim. Bracketed fields and `TBD—owner verification required` are not public copy. Use only public business contact data or permissioned records.

## Status
Requirements only; do not build or publish until owner, proof, privacy, technical and operational gates pass.
## Required modules
1. Audience-specific hero with bounded value proposition and approved CTA. 2. Suitable project types and explicit fit limits. 3. Process: intake, review, site walk trigger, proposal, coordination, changes and closeout. 4. Verified service/geography/capability facts. 5. Permissioned cases/proof. 6. Qualification form. 7. FAQ with no guarantees. 8. privacy/consent and contact details.
## Form requirements
Business/account, public business contact, role, project/location, service/scope, plans/spec availability, area/quantity if known, desired timing/bid date, occupancy/phasing, vendor/insurance/contract requirements, referral source, consent. Minimize sensitive data; secure uploads require a production security design. No form in this repo transmits.
## Measurement and QA
Track page → CTA → qualified form/call → account/opportunity → bid → won revenue/GP through approved consent and attribution architecture. Test mobile, accessibility, performance, validation, spam, privacy, routing and capacity pause. Never expose client/project data.
## Commercial-specific
+Address coordination, phasing, occupancy/downtime awareness, vendor-document review, finish/durability planning, escalation and closeout. Never imply bonding, union, insurance limits, certifications, after-hours capability or named commercial experience without proof.
