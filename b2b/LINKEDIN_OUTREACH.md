# Linkedin Outreach

> **Internal draft — owner approval required.** This is an operating specification, not authorization to contact, publish, connect a CRM, promise capacity, or make a public claim. Bracketed fields and `TBD—owner verification required` are not public copy. Use only public business contact data or permissioned records.

## Connection request
“[Name], [truthful shared context]. I work with NOLA Dustless and would value connecting around [specific hardwood/design/construction topic]. No pitch required.”
## After acceptance
Thank them; reference one relevant item; ask one fit question. Do not immediately paste a capability deck. Example: “Thanks, [Name]. I noticed [public business context]. When hardwood scopes arise, who usually owns early condition/scope review?”
## Limits
No automation, scraping, engagement bait, repeated DMs, personal-data enrichment or platform-limit workarounds. Stop after one unanswered follow-up; record permission and channel. Share proof only when approved and relevant.
