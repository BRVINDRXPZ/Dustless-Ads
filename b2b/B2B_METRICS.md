## Formulas
Win rate = awarded bids / decided bids under an owner-approved cohort rule. Average sales cycle = decision date − opportunity-created date. Revenue/account = reconciled revenue / revenue-producing accounts. Pipeline coverage = qualified open pipeline under an owner-approved stage/value rule divided by an approved future revenue target; leave blank until both exist. Weighted pipeline uses explicit provisional probability assumptions. Optimize for profitable, repeatable fit and cycle quality—not attempt volume.
