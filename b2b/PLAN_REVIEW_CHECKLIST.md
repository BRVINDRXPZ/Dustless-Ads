# Plan Review Checklist

> **Internal draft — owner approval required.** This is an operating specification, not authorization to contact, publish, connect a CRM, promise capacity, or make a public claim. Bracketed fields and `TBD—owner verification required` are not public copy. Use only public business contact data or permissioned records.

## Document control
- [ ] Drawing/spec/addendum names, dates and revisions logged; bid scope and alternates identified.
- [ ] Floor plans, finish schedule, details, elevations, transitions, stairs and demo sheets cross-checked.
## Scope takeoff
- [ ] Areas/quantities and measurement basis; material/species/grade/profile/dimensions/source; install/refinish/repair; pattern/layout; stairs; trim/base; transitions; thresholds; subfloor/prep; moisture/testing responsibility; adhesives/fasteners; sanding/stain/sealer/finish system; samples/mockups; protection/cleanup/disposal.
## Coordination
- [ ] Existing versus new; demolition; substrate responsibility; tolerances; acclimation/storage; HVAC/environment; sequencing and other trades; access/occupancy/phasing; cure/return-to-service per approved product data; owner-furnished material; waste; inspection/approval owners.
## Commercial terms and output
- [ ] Schedule/bid date; alternates/allowances; unit pricing only if approved; permits/vendor/safety; closeout; plan/spec conflicts and RFIs; assumptions, exclusions and unresolved gaps copied into proposal. Reviewer/date and go/clarify/no-bid recorded.
