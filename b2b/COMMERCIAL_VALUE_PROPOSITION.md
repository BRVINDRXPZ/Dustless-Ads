# Commercial Value Proposition

> **Internal draft — owner approval required.** This is an operating specification, not authorization to contact, publish, connect a CRM, promise capacity, or make a public claim. Bracketed fields and `TBD—owner verification required` are not public copy. Use only public business contact data or permissioned records.

## Position
A controlled hardwood workstream for suitable commercial projects: clarified scope, site coordination, phasing/occupancy planning, downtime awareness, finish-system planning, durability discussion, professional escalation and documented closeout.
## Buyer outcomes
Fewer unpriced gaps; visible constraints; explicit access/protection/other-trade dependencies; safer schedule decisions; organized submittal/product/SDS/maintenance records when applicable and available; named issue path and accountability.
## Boundaries
Bonding, union status, licenses, insurance limits, certifications, safety programs, after-hours ability and vendor documents are all `TBD—verification required`. Product performance follows manufacturer documentation; suitability requires project review.
