# Pipeline Stages

> **Internal draft — owner approval required.** This is an operating specification, not authorization to contact, publish, connect a CRM, promise capacity, or make a public claim. Bracketed fields and `TBD—owner verification required` are not public copy. Use only public business contact data or permissioned records.

## Stage dictionary
Probability is an owner-approved planning assumption stored on the **opportunity**, never an asserted fact. Account-only stages carry no revenue. Maximum idle periods below are proposed controls and may be revised.
### Target Identified
- **Entry:** documented evidence supports `Target Identified`; no stage skipping without required data. **Exit:** explicit next-stage criterion or terminal disposition is recorded.
- **Required:** account ID, segment/tier, role/source, permission, owner, evidence, next action.
- **Owner/action/idle:** relationship owner; complete the single dated next action; proposed maximum 90 days/review date.
- **Probability/revenue:** not applicable; zero pipeline revenue.
- **Loss:** required only for Lost/No Fit: controlled reason, evidence, lesson, corrective action and re-engagement date or never.
